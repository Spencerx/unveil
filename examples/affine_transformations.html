Challenge

Scale or rotate around a reference point rather than scaling or rotating around the origin. This is important for mouse interaction where you want to zoom or rotate based on the current mouse position. At the moment you can't combine those two transformations. Make sure either Zoom or Scale have a zero-value.

About

Unveil.js provides a simple abstraction layer for visualizations to ease the process of creating re-usable charts. To accomplish this, a data-driven methodology is used.
